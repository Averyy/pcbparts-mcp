Tools

Search parts across JLCPCB, Mouser, and DigiKey with 16 MCP tools. Compare stock and pricing across distributors, validate your BOM before ordering, export JLCPCB-compatible assembly CSVs, and download KiCad symbols and footprints.

🛠️

16 MCP Tools

jlc_search Parametric search with spec filters
jlc_search_api Live API search, real-time stock
jlc_get_part Details, datasheets, footprints
jlc_get_pinout Pin info from EasyEDA symbols
jlc_find_alternatives Spec-compatible alternative parts
jlc_validate_bom Check BOM stock and pricing
jlc_export_bom JLCPCB-compatible assembly CSV
jlc_list_categories Browse component categories
jlc_get_subcategories Subcategories within a category
jlc_list_attributes Filterable specs per subcategory
mouser_search Search parts on Mouser
mouser_get_part Mouser part lookup by PN
digikey_search Search parts on DigiKey
digikey_get_part DigiKey part lookup by PN
cse_search ECAD models and datasheets
cse_get_kicad KiCad symbols and footprints
